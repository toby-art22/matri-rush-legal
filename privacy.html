Privacy Policy

Matri Rush · Effective date: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how tobyart (“we”, “us”, “our”), the data controller, collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you play Matri Rush (the “Game”). Matri Rush is available to players worldwide. By playing, you agree to this policy.

Controller contact: tobyart, Ikeja, Lagos, Nigeria. Email amootobi22@gmail.com.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: if you create an account or sign in, the email address and provider identifier used to authenticate you. You can sign in with email, Google, Apple, or Facebook.
  • Game progress: your level progress, stars, hearts, Gold Bolts, boosters and settings, so they can be saved and synced across your devices.
  • Purchase data: when you buy in-app items, the app store (Google Play, or the Apple App Store where applicable) and our purchase processor handle the transaction; we receive a confirmation and a non-identifying purchase record. We never see your full payment card details.
  • Advertising data: the Game shows optional rewarded video ads (for example, watch an ad to earn a heart) through Google AdMob. When ads are served, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process your device advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA), IP address, general device and OS information, and data about your interaction with the ad, in order to serve, cap, measure, and, where you consent, personalize ads. See section 4.
  • Analytics & crash data: we use Google Firebase (Google Analytics for Firebase and Crashlytics) to understand how the Game is used and to diagnose crashes. This includes pseudonymous usage events, app and device information, a Firebase installation identifier, and crash diagnostics. It is not used to identify you personally.
  • Push notification data: if you allow notifications, we store a device push token (from Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification service) linked to your account so we can send in-game announcements. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.
  • Device & technical data: a device or installation identifier, IP address, and basic technical information (OS version, device model) needed to deliver and restore purchases, secure the Game, and keep it working.
  • Support messages: anything you send us directly.

2. How we use information

  • To run the Game and save/sync your progress.
  • To process and restore in-app purchases.
  • To serve, measure, and (with your consent) personalize rewarded ads.
  • To understand usage, fix bugs, and improve stability and performance.
  • To send in-game notifications you have opted into.
  • To provide support and respond to your requests.
  • To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and keep the Game secure.
  • To comply with our legal obligations (for example, tax and accounting for purchases).

4. Advertising & your ad choices

Ads keep Matri Rush free. In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we present a consent request (via Google’s User Messaging Platform) before any personalized ads are shown, and we respect your choice. If you do not consent, you will receive only non-personalized (contextual) ads.

You can change your ad choices at any time:

  • Reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).
  • Where a consent screen was shown, you can reopen it from the Game’s settings to withdraw or change consent.
  • California residents: see section 8 for the right to opt out of “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Legal bases for processing (EU/UK & Nigeria)

Where the GDPR, UK GDPR, or Nigeria’s Data Protection Act applies, we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract: to run the Game, authenticate you, sync your progress, and process purchases.
  • Consent: for personalized advertising and, where required, analytics. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the Game, prevent fraud, fix bugs, and improve performance (non-personalized ads and basic analytics where permitted).
  • Legal obligation: to meet tax, accounting, and other statutory duties.

6. Data retention

We keep account and cloud-save data for as long as your account exists. Game progress is also stored locally on your device. Analytics and crash data are retained on a rolling basis by our analytics provider. After you delete your account we remove your personal data, except a minimal, non-identifying record of completed purchases we must keep for tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention purposes. You can request deletion at any time (see section 8).

7. Children’s privacy

Matri Rush is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under the age of digital consent in their region (for example, 13 in the United States, or 13–16 in the EU/UK depending on the country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and our ads are not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact amootobi22@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate data;
  • delete your data or your entire account;
  • receive a portable copy of your data;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • withdraw consent (for ads or analytics) at any time;
  • not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

Delete your account: open Settings → Account & help in the Game and use the account-deletion option, or visit our account deletion page, or email us. We confirm and complete deletion within 30 days.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell your personal information for money. Because we serve personalized ads, our use of advertising identifiers may qualify as “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising under California law. You have the right to opt out of this sharing and to limit the use of sensitive personal information. To opt out, decline the ad-personalization consent request, limit ad tracking in your device settings, or email us at amootobi22@gmail.com with the subject “Do Not Sell or Share.” We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

EU, UK & Nigeria

You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority: in Nigeria, the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC); in the EU/UK, your national supervisory authority.

To exercise any right, email amootobi22@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.
