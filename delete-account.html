Matri Rush · How to delete your account and data

You can ask us to delete your Matri Rush account and the data linked to it at any time. You don't need to keep the app installed to make this request.

How to request deletion

Email support@matrirush.com from any address with the subject “Delete my Matri Rush account” and include your User ID. You'll find it in the app under Settings → Account & help → User ID (tap Copy). If you can't open the app, tell us the email address you signed in with instead.

Email a deletion request

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in email (and any Google/Facebook/Apple link).
  • Your cloud save: level progress, stars, hearts, Gold Bolts, boosters and settings.

What we keep

We retain a minimal, non-identifying record of completed purchases where we're required to for tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention purposes. This record is not used to identify you after deletion.

How long it takes

We confirm and complete deletion within 30 days of your request, and usually much sooner. Deletion is permanent and can't be undone.
